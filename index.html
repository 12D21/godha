About

"The "GODHA" Sports Meet 2022-23, organized by the Maunjathul Afnan Students Association, is an exciting event that aims to bring back and promote traditional sports such as Cattippanthu Vadamvali etc.

Not only will this event provide a platform for participants to showcase their skills in these unique games, but it will also serve as an opportunity to improve physical abilities and fitness among students.
